## Why I Built TikT Downloader

The idea came from a personal experience. One day I was scrolling through TikTok and came across a powerful motivational video that really resonated with me. I wanted to save it — but the creator had disabled the download button.

I started searching for a downloader online. Most of what I found was either too slow, covered in annoying ads, or added an ugly watermark to every video. None of them felt clean or trustworthy.

That moment sparked the idea. I thought — what if there was a simple, fast, and honest tool that just works? No watermark. No sign-up. No confusion. Just paste your link and download.

That is exactly what TikT Downloader is.
